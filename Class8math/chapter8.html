Exercise 10.1

1. Evaluate.

(i) 3⁻² = 1 / 3² = 1/9
(ii) (-4)⁻² = 1 / (-4)² = 1/16
(iii) (1/2)⁻⁵ = (2/1)⁵ = 2⁵ = 32

Hinglish Explanation:

Rule: Agar power negative hai (jaise a⁻ⁿ), to use positive banane ke liye number ka reciprocal (ulta) kar do (1/aⁿ). Agar fraction ki power negative hai (jaise (a/b)⁻ⁿ), to fraction ko ulta kar do aur power positive ho jayegi ((b/a)ⁿ).

2. Simplify and express the result in power notation with positive exponent.

(i) (-4)⁵ ÷ (-4)⁸ = (-4)⁵⁻⁸ = (-4)⁻³ = 1/(-4)³
(ii) (1/2³) ² = 1² / (2³)² = 1 / 2⁶
(iii) (-3)⁴ × (5/3)⁴ = (-1 × 3)⁴ × (5⁴/3⁴) = (-1)⁴ × 3⁴ × 5⁴ / 3⁴ = 1 × 5⁴ = 5⁴
(iv) (3⁻⁷ ÷ 3⁻¹⁰) × 3⁻⁵ = (3⁻⁷⁻⁽⁻¹⁰⁾) × 3⁻⁵ = 3³ × 3⁻⁵ = 3³⁻⁵ = 3⁻² = 1/3²
(v) 2⁻³ × (-7)⁻³ = (2 × -7)⁻³ = (-14)⁻³ = 1/(-14)³

Hinglish Explanation:

Yahan laws of exponents use honge: Divide (i, iv): Base same ho to powers subtract hoti hain. Power of power (ii): Bracket ke andar aur bahar ki power multiply hoti hain. Power same (iii, v): Base alag ho par power same ho to bases ko multiply karke common power laga do.

3. Find the value of.

(i) (3⁰ + 4⁻¹) × 2² = (1 + 1/4) × 4 = (5/4) × 4 = 5
(ii) (2⁻¹ × 4⁻¹) ÷ 2⁻² = (1/2 × 1/4) ÷ (1/2²) = (1/8) ÷ (1/4) = 1/8 × 4/1 = 1/2
(iii) (1/2)⁻² + (1/3)⁻² + (1/4)⁻² = 2² + 3² + 4² = 4 + 9 + 16 = 29
(iv) (3⁻¹ + 4⁻¹ + 5⁻¹)⁰ = 1 (Kisi bhi non-zero number ki power 0 hamesha 1 hoti hai)
(v) {(-2/3)⁻²}² = (-2/3)⁻⁴ = (-3/2)⁴ = 81/16

Hinglish Explanation:

Pehle brackets ke andar simplify karo. Negative powers ko positive banao, fractions ko solve karo. Yaad rakho a⁰ = 1. Fir final calculation karo.

4. Evaluate.

(i) (8⁻¹ × 5³) / 2⁻⁴ = ( (2³)⁻¹ × 5³ ) / 2⁻⁴ = ( 2⁻³ × 5³ ) / 2⁻⁴ = 2⁻³⁻⁽⁻⁴⁾ × 5³ = 2¹ × 5³ = 2 × 125 = 250
(ii) (5⁻¹ × 2⁻¹) × 6⁻¹ = ( (5 × 2)⁻¹ ) × 6⁻¹ = 10⁻¹ × 6⁻¹ = (10 × 6)⁻¹ = 60⁻¹ = 1/60

Hinglish Explanation:

(i): 8 ko 2³ likho. Fir power rules apply karke base '2' ki powers ko solve karo. (ii): 5⁻¹ aur 2⁻¹ ko combine karke 10⁻¹ likh sakte hain, fir use 6⁻¹ ke saath combine karo.

5. Find the value of m for which 5ᵐ ÷ 5⁻³ = 5⁵.

Using the law aᵐ ÷ aⁿ = aᵐ⁻ⁿ, we get: 5ᵐ⁻⁽⁻³⁾ = 5⁵
5ᵐ⁺³ = 5⁵
Since the bases are equal, the exponents must be equal. So, m + 3 = 5.
m = 5 - 3 = 2.

m = 2

Hinglish Explanation:

Jab equation ke dono taraf base same ho (yahan 5 hai), to hum unki powers ko equal kar sakte hain. Divide rule se m - (-3) = m+3 ho gaya. To m+3 = 5, jisse m=2 milta hai.

6. Evaluate.

(i) {(1/3)⁻¹ - (1/4)⁻¹}⁻¹ = {3 - 4}⁻¹ = (-1)⁻¹ = 1/(-1) = -1
(ii) (5/8)⁻⁷ × (8/5)⁻⁴ = (5/8)⁻⁷ × (5/8)⁴ = (5/8)⁻⁷⁺⁴ = (5/8)⁻³ = (8/5)³ = 512/125

Hinglish Explanation:

(i): Pehle bracket ke andar negative powers ko solve karo (reciprocal leke). Fir subtract karke jo answer aaye, uski power -1 solve karo. (ii): (8/5)⁻⁴ ko (5/8)⁴ likh sakte hain taaki base same ho jaye. Fir powers ko add kar do.

7. Simplify.

(i) (25 × t⁻⁴) / (5⁻³ × 10 × t⁻⁸)
= (5² × t⁻⁴) / (5⁻³ × 2 × 5¹ × t⁻⁸)
= (5² × t⁻⁴) / (5⁻² × 2 × t⁻⁸)
= (5²⁻⁽⁻²⁾ × t⁻⁴⁻⁽⁻⁸⁾) / 2 = (5⁴ × t⁴) / 2 = 625t⁴/2
(ii) (3⁻⁵ × 10⁻⁵ × 125) / (5⁻⁷ × 6⁻⁵)
= (3⁻⁵ × (2×5)⁻⁵ × 5³) / (5⁻⁷ × (2×3)⁻⁵)
= (3⁻⁵ × 2⁻⁵ × 5⁻⁵ × 5³) / (5⁻⁷ × 2⁻⁵ × 3⁻⁵)
= 3⁻⁵⁻⁽⁻⁵⁾ × 2⁻⁵⁻⁽⁻⁵⁾ × 5⁻²⁻⁽⁻⁷⁾ = 3⁰ × 2⁰ × 5⁵ = 1 × 1 × 5⁵ = 3125

Hinglish Explanation:

Sabhi numbers ko unke prime factors (jaise 25=5², 10=2×5) me break kar lo. Fir same base waali powers ko division (subtract) aur multiplication (add) ke rules se solve karo.

Exercise 10.2

1. Express the following numbers in standard form.

(i) 0.0000000000085 = 8.5 × 10⁻¹²
(ii) 0.00000000000942 = 9.42 × 10⁻¹²
(iii) 6020000000000000 = 6.02 × 10¹⁵
(iv) 0.00000000837 = 8.37 × 10⁻⁹
(v) 31860000000 = 3.186 × 10¹⁰

Hinglish Explanation:

Standard form me likhne ke liye, decimal point ko pehli non-zero digit ke baad lagao. Small numbers (0.00...): Jitne places decimal ko right me move kiya, 10 ki power utni hi negative ho jayegi. Large numbers: Jitne places decimal ko left me move kiya, 10 ki power utni hi positive ho jayegi.

2. Express the following numbers in usual form.

(i) 3.02 × 10⁻⁶ = 0.00000302
(ii) 4.5 × 10⁴ = 45000
(iii) 3 × 10⁻⁸ = 0.00000003
(iv) 1.0001 × 10⁹ = 1000100000
(v) 5.8 × 10¹² = 5800000000000
(vi) 3.61492 × 10⁶ = 3614920

Hinglish Explanation:

Negative Power (10⁻ⁿ): Decimal point ko left me 'n' places move karo. Positive Power (10ⁿ): Decimal point ko right me 'n' places move karo. Zaroorat padne par extra zeros lagao.

3. Express the number appearing in the following statements in standard form.

(i) 1 micron is equal to 1/1000000 m = 1/10⁶ m = 1 × 10⁻⁶ m.
(ii) Charge of an electron is 0.000,000,000,000,000,000,16 coulomb = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C.
(iii) Size of a bacteria is 0.0000005 m = 5 × 10⁻⁷ m.
(iv) Size of a plant cell is 0.00001275 m = 1.275 × 10⁻⁵ m.
(v) Thickness of a thick paper is 0.07 mm = 7 × 10⁻² mm.

Hinglish Explanation:

Bilkul Question 1 ki tarah, in sabhi numbers ko standard form me convert karna hai. Decimal point ko pehli non-zero digit ke baad shift karo aur count karo kitne places shift kiya. Yahan sabhi numbers chhote hain, isliye power negative aayegi.

4. In a stack there are 5 books each of thickness 20mm and 5 paper sheets each of thickness 0.016 mm. What is the total thickness of the stack.

Thickness of 5 books = 5 × 20 mm = 100 mm.
Thickness of 5 paper sheets = 5 × 0.016 mm = 0.080 mm.
Total thickness = 100 mm + 0.080 mm = 100.08 mm.
In standard form: 100.08 = 1.0008 × 10² mm.

Total thickness = 1.0008 × 10² mm

Hinglish Explanation:

Pehle 5 books ki total motai (thickness) nikalo (5 × 20 = 100). Fir 5 papers ki total motai nikalo (5 × 0.016 = 0.08). Dono ko add kar do (100 + 0.08 = 100.08). Fir is final answer ko standard form me likh do.

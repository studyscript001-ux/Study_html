1. Find the ratio of the following.

(a) Speed of a cycle 15 km per hour to the speed of scooter 30 km per hour.
Ratio = 15:30. Simplifying this by dividing both sides by 15, we get 1:2.
(b) 5 m to 10 km.
First, convert both quantities to the same unit. 10 km = 10 * 1000 m = 10000 m.
Ratio = 5:10000. Simplifying by dividing both sides by 5, we get 1:2000.
(c) 50 paise to ₹ 5.
Convert to the same unit. ₹ 5 = 5 * 100 paise = 500 paise.
Ratio = 50:500. Simplifying by dividing both sides by 50, we get 1:10.

Hinglish Explanation:

Ratio nikalne ke liye, dono quantities ka unit same hona chahiye. (a) me unit pehle se same hai (km/hr), to seedhe 15/30 = 1/2. (b) me km ko meter me badla (10km = 10000m). (c) me Rupee ko paise me badla (₹5 = 500p). Fir simplify kiya.

1. During a sale, a shop offered a discount of 10% on the marked prices of all the items. What would a customer have to pay for a pair of jeans marked at ₹ 1450 and two shirts marked at ₹ 850 each?

Marked Price (MP) of jeans = ₹ 1450. MP of two shirts = 2 * 850 = ₹ 1700.
Total MP = 1450 + 1700 = ₹ 3150.
Discount = 10% of 3150 = (10/100) * 3150 = ₹ 315.
Selling Price (SP) = MP - Discount = 3150 - 315 = ₹ 2835.

The customer would have to pay ₹ 2835.

Hinglish Explanation:

Pehle sabhi items ka total Marked Price (MP) nikalo (Jeans + 2 Shirts). Fir us total amount par 10% discount calculate karo. Aakhir me, Total MP me se discount minus kar do to final payment amount mil jayega.

3. Arun bought a pair of skates at a sale where the discount given was 20%. If the amount he pays is ₹ 1,600, find the marked price.

Arun paid ₹ 1600 after a 20% discount. This means he paid 100% - 20% = 80% of the marked price.
Let the Marked Price (MP) be x. So, 80% of x = 1600.
(80/100) * x = 1600.
x = (1600 * 100) / 80 = ₹ 2000.

The marked price was ₹ 2000.

Hinglish Explanation:

20% discount ke baad usne ₹1600 diye, iska matlab usne Marked Price (MP) ka 80% pay kiya. Agar 80% = ₹1600 hai, to 100% (poora MP) kitna hoga? Unitary method se solve karo: (1600 / 80) * 100 = ₹2000.

1. The population of a place increased to 54,000 in 2003 at a rate of 5% per annum.

(i) find the population in 2001.

This is like finding the Principal when the Amount is given (reverse calculation). Let population in 2001 be P.
Population in 2003 (A) = P * (1 + R/100)^n
54000 = P * (1 + 5/100)² = P * (21/20)²
P = 54000 * (20/21)² = 54000 * (400/441) ≈ 48979.59. So, population was approx 48,980.

(ii) what would be its population in 2005?

Here, the base population is 54,000 (from 2003) and we need to find for 2005 (2 years later).
Population in 2005 = 54000 * (1 + 5/100)²
= 54000 * (21/20)² = 54000 * (441/400) = 59,535.

Hinglish Explanation:

(i) 2001 ki population: Yeh compound interest ka ulta सवाल hai. Humein 2003 ki population (Amount) pata hai aur 2001 ki (Principal) nikalni hai. Formula me values daal kar P ke liye solve karo.
(ii) 2005 ki population: Yeh seedha सवाल hai. 2003 ki population ko Principal maano aur 2 saal (2005 tak) ka 5% rate se Amount (final population) nikal lo.

2. In a Laboratory, the count of bacteria in a certain experiment was increasing at the rate of 2.5% per hour. Find the bacteria at the end of 2 hours if the count was initially 5,06,000.

Initial count (P) = 5,06,000. Rate of increase (R) = 2.5% per hour. Time (n) = 2 hours.
Using the compound interest formula: A = P * (1 + R/100)^n
A = 506000 * (1 + 2.5/100)² = 506000 * (1 + 1/40)²
A = 506000 * (41/40)² = 506000 * (1681/1600) = 531616.25

The count of bacteria after 2 hours will be approximately 5,31,616.

Hinglish Explanation:

Yeh bilkul compound interest jaisa hai, jahan Principal (P) shuru ke bacteria hain, Rate (R) unke badhne ka rate hai, aur Time (n) 2 ghante hai. Formula A = P(1 + R/100)^n me saari values daal kar final Amount (A) nikal lo.

3. A scooter was bought at ₹ 42,000. Its value depreciated at the rate of 8% per annum. Find its value after one year.

Depreciation means the value decreases. We use the formula A = P * (1 - R/100)^n.
P = 42000, R = 8%, n = 1 year.
Value after 1 year = 42000 * (1 - 8/100)¹ = 42000 * (92/100) = ₹ 38,640.

Hinglish Explanation:

Depreciation ka matlab hai value ka kam hona. Isliye compound interest ke formula me plus (+) ki jagah minus (-) lagayenge. P = 42000, R = 8, n = 1 daal kar solve karo.

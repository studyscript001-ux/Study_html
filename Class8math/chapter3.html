2. What is a regular polygon? State the name of a regular polygon of (i) 3 sides (ii) 4 sides (iii) 6 sides.

Solution:

A regular polygon is both equiangular (all angles equal) and equilateral (all sides equal).

  • (i) 3 sides: Equilateral Triangle
  • (ii) 4 sides: Square
  • (iii) 6 sides: Regular Hexagon

Hinglish Explanation:

Regular polygon woh hai jiski saari sides aur saare angles barabar hote hain.

2. Find the measure of each exterior angle of a regular polygon of (i) 9 sides (ii) 15 sides.

Solution:

Formula: Each exterior angle = 360° / number of sides.

(i) 9 sides: 360° / 9 = 40°.

(ii) 15 sides: 360° / 15 = 24°.

3. How many sides does a regular polygon have if the measure of an exterior angle is 24°?

Solution:

Formula: Number of sides = 360° / exterior angle.

Number of sides = 360° / 24° = 15.

4. How many sides does a regular polygon have if each of its interior angles is 165°?

Solution:

Exterior Angle = 180° - Interior Angle = 180° - 165° = 15°.

Number of sides = 360° / 15° = 24.

Hinglish Explanation:

Step 1: Exterior Angle nikalo (180° - 165° = 15°). Step 2: 360° ko exterior angle se divide karo (360/15 = 24).

5. (a) Is it possible to have a regular polygon with measure of each exterior angle as 22°? (b) Can it be an interior angle of a regular polygon? Why?

Solution:

(a) No. 360 is not divisible by 22 (360/22 = 16.36).

(b) No. If interior is 22°, exterior is 158°. 360 is not divisible by 158.

Hinglish Explanation:

Sides ka number hamesha whole number hona chahiye. 360 ko exterior angle se poori tarah divide hona zaroori hai.

6. (a) What is the minimum interior angle possible for a regular polygon? (b) What is the maximum exterior angle possible for a regular polygon?

Solution:

(a) 60°. The polygon with the minimum sides (3) is an equilateral triangle, which has 60° angles.

(b) 120°. It corresponds to the equilateral triangle (180° - 60° = 120°).

6. ABC is a right-angled triangle and O is the mid point of the side opposite to the right angle. Explain why O is equidistant from A, B and C.

Right-angled Triangle ABC

Solution:

Complete the figure to form a rectangle ABCD. In a rectangle, diagonals are equal (AC=BD) and bisect each other at O. This means OA = OC = OB = OD. Therefore, O is equidistant from A, B, and C.

Hinglish Explanation:

Is figure ko ek rectangle (aayat) complete karo. Rectangle ke diagonals barabar hote hain aur O unka midpoint hota hai. Isliye O se A, B, aur C ki doori barabar hogi (OA=OB=OC).

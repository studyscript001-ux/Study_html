Exercise 2.1

1. Solve: 3x = 2x + 18

Transpose 2x from the right side (RHS) to the left side (LHS). It becomes -2x.
3x - 2x = 18
Simplify the expression on the LHS.
x = 18

Answer: x = 18

2. Solve: 5t - 3 = 3t - 5

Transpose 3t from RHS to LHS (becomes -3t) and -3 from LHS to RHS (becomes +3).
5t - 3t = -5 + 3
Simplify both sides.
2t = -2
Divide both sides by 2.
t = -2 / 2

Answer: t = -1

3. Solve: 5x + 9 = 5 + 3x

Transpose 3x from RHS to LHS (becomes -3x) and 9 from LHS to RHS (becomes -9).
5x - 3x = 5 - 9
Simplify both sides.
2x = -4
Divide both sides by 2.
x = -4 / 2

Answer: x = -2

4. Solve: 4z + 3 = 6 + 2z

Transpose 2z from RHS to LHS and 3 from LHS to RHS.
4z - 2z = 6 - 3
Simplify both sides.
2z = 3
Divide both sides by 2.

Answer: z = 3/2

5. Solve: 2x - 1 = 14 - x

Transpose -x from RHS to LHS (becomes +x) and -1 from LHS to RHS (becomes +1).
2x + x = 14 + 1
Simplify both sides.
3x = 15
Divide both sides by 3.

Answer: x = 5

6. Solve: 8x + 4 = 3(x - 1) + 7

First, open the bracket on the RHS: 3 * x - 3 * 1 = 3x - 3.
The equation becomes: 8x + 4 = 3x - 3 + 7
Combine the constant terms on the RHS: -3 + 7 = 4.
Equation is now: 8x + 4 = 3x + 4
Transpose 3x to LHS and 4 to RHS.
8x - 3x = 4 - 4
5x = 0
Divide by 5.

Answer: x = 0

7. Solve: x = 4/5 (x + 10)

Multiply both sides by 5 to remove the denominator.
5 * x = 5 * [4/5 (x + 10)]
5x = 4(x + 10)
Open the bracket: 4 * x + 4 * 10.
5x = 4x + 40
Transpose 4x to the LHS.
5x - 4x = 40

Answer: x = 40

8. Solve: 2x/3 + 1 = 7x/15 + 3

Transpose 7x/15 to LHS and 1 to RHS.
2x/3 - 7x/15 = 3 - 1
The LCM of denominators 3 and 15 is 15.
(5 * 2x - 1 * 7x) / 15 = 2
(10x - 7x) / 15 = 2
3x / 15 = 2
Multiply both sides by 15.
3x = 30
Divide by 3.

Answer: x = 10

9. Solve: 2y + 5/3 = 26/3 - y

Transpose -y to LHS and 5/3 to RHS.
2y + y = 26/3 - 5/3
Simplify both sides. The denominators on the RHS are the same.
3y = (26 - 5) / 3
3y = 21 / 3
3y = 7
Divide by 3.

Answer: y = 7/3

10. Solve: 3m = 5m - 8/5

Transpose 5m to LHS.
3m - 5m = -8/5
-2m = -8/5
The negative signs on both sides cancel out.
2m = 8/5
Divide both sides by 2.
m = (8/5) / 2 = 8 / (5 * 2) = 8 / 10
Simplify the fraction.

Answer: m = 4/5

Exercise 2.2

1. Solve: x/2 - 1/5 = x/3 + 1/4

Group the variable terms on LHS and constant terms on RHS.
x/2 - x/3 = 1/4 + 1/5
On LHS, LCM of 2 and 3 is 6. On RHS, LCM of 4 and 5 is 20.
(3x - 2x)/6 = (5 + 4)/20
x/6 = 9/20
Multiply both sides by 6.
x = (9 * 6) / 20 = 54 / 20
Simplify the fraction by dividing numerator and denominator by 2.

Answer: x = 27/10

2. Solve: n/2 - 3n/4 + 5n/6 = 21

The variable terms are already on the LHS.
Find the LCM of the denominators 2, 4, and 6. The LCM is 12.
(6*n - 3*3n + 2*5n) / 12 = 21
(6n - 9n + 10n) / 12 = 21
Simplify the numerator: (16n - 9n) / 12 = 21
7n / 12 = 21
Multiply both sides by 12.
7n = 21 * 12
7n = 252
Divide both sides by 7.

Answer: n = 36

3. Solve: x + 7 - 8x/3 = 17/6 - 5x/2

Group variable terms on LHS and constants on RHS.
x - 8x/3 + 5x/2 = 17/6 - 7
On LHS, LCM of 1, 3, 2 is 6. On RHS, LCM of 6, 1 is 6.
(6x - 2*8x + 3*5x)/6 = (17 - 6*7)/6
(6x - 16x + 15x)/6 = (17 - 42)/6
(21x - 16x)/6 = -25/6
5x/6 = -25/6
Multiply both sides by 6 to cancel denominators.
5x = -25
Divide by 5.

Answer: x = -5

4. Solve: (x - 5)/3 = (x - 3)/5

Cross-multiply the terms.
5 * (x - 5) = 3 * (x - 3)
Open the brackets on both sides.
5x - 25 = 3x - 9
Transpose 3x to LHS and -25 to RHS.
5x - 3x = -9 + 25
2x = 16
Divide by 2.

Answer: x = 8

5. Solve: (3t - 2)/4 - (2t + 3)/3 = 2/3 - t

Move -t to LHS. It becomes +t.
(3t - 2)/4 - (2t + 3)/3 + t = 2/3
LCM of 4 and 3 on the LHS is 12.
[3(3t - 2) - 4(2t + 3) + 12t] / 12 = 2/3
(9t - 6 - 8t - 12 + 12t) / 12 = 2/3
Combine like terms in the numerator: (13t - 18) / 12 = 2/3
Multiply both sides by 12.
13t - 18 = (2/3) * 12 = 8
13t = 8 + 18
13t = 26
Divide by 13.

Answer: t = 2

6. Solve: m - (m - 1)/2 = 1 - (m - 2)/3

Group variable terms on LHS.
m - (m - 1)/2 + (m - 2)/3 = 1
LCM of 2 and 3 is 6.
[6m - 3(m - 1) + 2(m - 2)] / 6 = 1
(6m - 3m + 3 + 2m - 4) / 6 = 1
Combine like terms in numerator: (5m - 1) / 6 = 1
Multiply both sides by 6.
5m - 1 = 6
5m = 7

Answer: m = 7/5

7. Simplify and solve: 3(t - 3) = 5(2t + 1)

Open brackets on both sides.
3t - 9 = 10t + 5
Transpose 10t to LHS and -9 to RHS.
3t - 10t = 5 + 9
-7t = 14
Divide by -7.

Answer: t = -2

8. Simplify and solve: 15(y - 4) - 2(y - 9) + 5(y + 6) = 0

Open all the brackets.
15y - 60 - 2y + 18 + 5y + 30 = 0
Combine 'y' terms: 15y - 2y + 5y = 18y.
Combine constant terms: -60 + 18 + 30 = -12.
The equation becomes: 18y - 12 = 0
18y = 12
y = 12 / 18
Simplify the fraction by dividing by 6.

Answer: y = 2/3

9. Simplify and solve: 3(5z - 7) - 2(9z - 11) = 4(8z - 13) - 17

Simplify LHS: 15z - 21 - 18z + 22 which is -3z + 1.
Simplify RHS: 32z - 52 - 17 which is 32z - 69.
The equation is now: -3z + 1 = 32z - 69
Transpose 32z to LHS and 1 to RHS.
-3z - 32z = -69 - 1
-35z = -70
Divide by -35.

Answer: z = 2

10. Simplify and solve: 0.25(4f - 3) = 0.05(10f - 9)

Open the brackets on both sides.
1f - 0.75 = 0.5f - 0.45
Transpose 0.5f to LHS and -0.75 to RHS.
f - 0.5f = -0.45 + 0.75
0.5f = 0.30
Divide by 0.5.
f = 0.30 / 0.5

Answer: f = 0.6

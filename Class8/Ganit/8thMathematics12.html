Exercise 12.1

Question 1: Find the common factors of the given terms:

Solution:

(i) 12x, 36

12x = 2 × 2 × 3 × x

36 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3

Common factor = 2 × 2 × 3 = 12

Answer: 12

Hinglish Explanation: Humne 12x aur 36 ko unke prime factors mein break kiya. Phir common factors ko multiply karke 12 common factor prapt hua.

(ii) 2y, 22xy

2y = 2 × y

22xy = 2 × 11 × x × y

Common factor = 2 × y = 2y

Answer: 2y

Hinglish Explanation: Dono terms ko prime factors mein break karke, common factors 2 aur y ko multiply karke 2y common factor mila.

(iii) 14pq, 28p²q²

14pq = 2 × 7 × p × q

28p²q² = 2 × 2 × 7 × p × p × q × q

Common factor = 2 × 7 × p × q = 14pq

Answer: 14pq

Hinglish Explanation: Prime factorization karne ke baad, common factors 2, 7, p aur q ko multiply karke 14pq common factor mila.

(iv) 2x, 3x², 4

2x = 2 × x

3x² = 3 × x × x

4 = 2 × 2

Common factor = 1 (only 1 is the common factor)

Answer: 1

Hinglish Explanation: Teenon terms ke prime factors mein koi common factor nahi hai, isliye 1 hi common factor hai.

(v) 6abc, 24ab², 12a²b

6abc = 2 × 3 × a × b × c

24ab² = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × a × b × b

12a²b = 2 × 2 × 3 × a × a × b

Common factor = 2 × 3 × a × b = 6ab

Answer: 6ab

Hinglish Explanation: Teen terms ke prime factors nikalne ke baad, common factors 2, 3, a aur b ko multiply karke 6ab common factor mila.

(vi) 16x³, -4x², 32x

16x³ = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × x × x × x

-4x² = -1 × 2 × 2 × x × x

32x = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × x

Common factor = 2 × 2 × x = 4x

Answer: 4x

Hinglish Explanation: Negative sign ko ignore karke prime factors nikalne par, common factors 2, 2 aur x multiply karke 4x mila.

(vii) 10pq, 20qr, 30rp

10pq = 2 × 5 × p × q

20qr = 2 × 2 × 5 × q × r

30rp = 2 × 3 × 5 × r × p

Common factor = 2 × 5 = 10

Answer: 10

Hinglish Explanation: Teen terms mein sirf 2 aur 5 common factors hain, unko multiply karke 10 common factor mila.

(viii) 3x²y³, 10x³y², 6x²y²z

3x²y³ = 3 × x × x × y × y × y

10x³y² = 2 × 5 × x × x × x × y × y

6x²y²z = 2 × 3 × x × x × y × y × z

Common factor = x × x × y × y = x²y²

Answer: x²y²

Hinglish Explanation: Teen terms mein x² aur y² common hain, isliye common factor x²y² hai.

Question 2: Factorise the following expressions:

Solution:

(i) 7x - 42

7x = 7 × x

42 = 7 × 6

Common factor = 7

7x - 42 = 7(x - 6)

Answer: 7(x - 6)

Hinglish Explanation: 7 common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (x - 6) bacha.

(ii) 6p - 12q

6p = 2 × 3 × p

12q = 2 × 2 × 3 × q

Common factor = 2 × 3 = 6

6p - 12q = 6(p - 2q)

Answer: 6(p - 2q)

Hinglish Explanation: 6 common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (p - 2q) bacha.

(iii) 7a² + 14a

7a² = 7 × a × a

14a = 2 × 7 × a

Common factor = 7 × a = 7a

7a² + 14a = 7a(a + 2)

Answer: 7a(a + 2)

Hinglish Explanation: 7a common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (a + 2) bacha.

(iv) -16z + 20z³

-16z = -1 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × z

20z³ = 2 × 2 × 5 × z × z × z

Common factor = 2 × 2 × z = 4z

-16z + 20z³ = 4z(-4 + 5z²)

Answer: 4z(5z² - 4)

Hinglish Explanation: 4z common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (-4 + 5z²) bacha, jise rearrange karke (5z² - 4) likh sakte hain.

(v) 20l²m + 30alm

20l²m = 2 × 2 × 5 × l × l × m

30alm = 2 × 3 × 5 × a × l × m

Common factor = 2 × 5 × l × m = 10lm

20l²m + 30alm = 10lm(2l + 3a)

Answer: 10lm(2l + 3a)

Hinglish Explanation: 10lm common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (2l + 3a) bacha.

(vi) 5x²y - 15xy²

5x²y = 5 × x × x × y

15xy² = 3 × 5 × x × y × y

Common factor = 5 × x × y = 5xy

5x²y - 15xy² = 5xy(x - 3y)

Answer: 5xy(x - 3y)

Hinglish Explanation: 5xy common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (x - 3y) bacha.

(vii) 10a² - 15b² + 20c²

10a² = 2 × 5 × a × a

15b² = 3 × 5 × b × b

20c² = 2 × 2 × 5 × c × c

Common factor = 5

10a² - 15b² + 20c² = 5(2a² - 3b² + 4c²)

Answer: 5(2a² - 3b² + 4c²)

Hinglish Explanation: Sirf 5 hi common factor hai, isliye 5 nikalne ke baad bracket mein (2a² - 3b² + 4c²) bacha.

(viii) -4a² + 4ab - 4ca

-4a² = -1 × 2 × 2 × a × a

4ab = 2 × 2 × a × b

4ca = 2 × 2 × c × a

Common factor = 2 × 2 × a = 4a

-4a² + 4ab - 4ca = 4a(-a + b - c)

Answer: 4a(-a + b - c) or -4a(a - b + c)

Hinglish Explanation: 4a common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (-a + b - c) bacha. Negative sign ko bracket ke bahar nikal kar -4a(a - b + c) bhi likh sakte hain.

(ix) x²yz + xy²z + xyz²

x²yz = x × x × y × z

xy²z = x × y × y × z

xyz² = x × y × z × z

Common factor = x × y × z = xyz

x²yz + xy²z + xyz² = xyz(x + y + z)

Answer: xyz(x + y + z)

Hinglish Explanation: xyz common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (x + y + z) bacha.

(x) ax²y + bxy² + cxyz

ax²y = a × x × x × y

bxy² = b × x × y × y

cxyz = c × x × y × z

Common factor = x × y = xy

ax²y + bxy² + cxyz = xy(ax + by + cz)

Answer: xy(ax + by + cz)

Hinglish Explanation: xy common factor nikalne ke baad, bracket mein (ax + by + cz) bacha.

Question 3: Factorise:

Solution:

(i) x² + xy + 8x + 8y

Grouping: (x² + xy) + (8x + 8y)

x(x + y) + 8(x + y)

(x + y)(x + 8)

Answer: (x + y)(x + 8)

Hinglish Explanation: Humne terms ko group kiya. Pehle group se x common liya, dusre group se 8 common liya. Dono groups mein (x + y) common tha, isliye final answer (x + y)(x + 8) mila.

(ii) 15xy - 6x + 5y - 2

Grouping: (15xy - 6x) + (5y - 2)

3x(5y - 2) + 1(5y - 2)

(5y - 2)(3x + 1)

Answer: (5y - 2)(3x + 1)

Hinglish Explanation: Grouping karne ke baad, pehle group se 3x common liya, dusre group se 1 common liya. Dono groups mein (5y - 2) common tha, isliye final answer (5y - 2)(3x + 1) mila.

(iii) ax + bx - ay - by

Grouping: (ax + bx) - (ay + by)

x(a + b) - y(a + b)

(a + b)(x - y)

Answer: (a + b)(x - y)

Hinglish Explanation: Grouping karne ke baad, pehle group se x common liya, dusre group se y common liya. Dono groups mein (a + b) common tha, isliye final answer (a + b)(x - y) mila.

(iv) 15pq + 15 + 9q + 25p

Rearranging: 15pq + 9q + 25p + 15

Grouping: (15pq + 9q) + (25p + 15)

3q(5p + 3) + 5(5p + 3)

(5p + 3)(3q + 5)

Answer: (5p + 3)(3q + 5)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle terms ko rearrange kiya, phir grouping karke pehle group se 3q common liya, dusre group se 5 common liya. Dono groups mein (5p + 3) common tha, isliye final answer (5p + 3)(3q + 5) mila.

(v) z - 7 + 7xy - xyz

Rearranging: -xyz + 7xy + z - 7

Grouping: (-xyz + 7xy) + (z - 7)

-xy(z - 7) + 1(z - 7)

(z - 7)(1 - xy)

Answer: (z - 7)(1 - xy)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle terms ko rearrange kiya, phir grouping karke pehle group se -xy common liya, dusre group se 1 common liya. Dono groups mein (z - 7) common tha, isliye final answer (z - 7)(1 - xy) mila.

Exercise 12.2

Question 1: Factorise the following squares using identities:

Solution:

(i) a² + 8a + 16

This is in the form (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²

Here a = a, b = 4

a² + 8a + 16 = (a + 4)²

Answer: (a + 4)²

Hinglish Explanation: Ye perfect square hai. (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b² identity use karke, a = a aur b = 4 rakhne par (a + 4)² milta hai.

(ii) p² - 10p + 25

This is in the form (a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b²

Here a = p, b = 5

p² - 10p + 25 = (p - 5)²

Answer: (p - 5)²

Hinglish Explanation: Ye perfect square hai. (a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b² identity use karke, a = p aur b = 5 rakhne par (p - 5)² milta hai.

(iii) 25m² + 30m + 9

This is in the form (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²

Here a = 5m, b = 3

25m² + 30m + 9 = (5m + 3)²

Answer: (5m + 3)²

Hinglish Explanation: Ye perfect square hai. (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b² identity use karke, a = 5m aur b = 3 rakhne par (5m + 3)² milta hai.

(iv) 49y² + 84yz + 36z²

This is in the form (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²

Here a = 7y, b = 6z

49y² + 84yz + 36z² = (7y + 6z)²

Answer: (7y + 6z)²

Hinglish Explanation: Ye perfect square hai. (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b² identity use karke, a = 7y aur b = 6z rakhne par (7y + 6z)² milta hai.

(v) 4x² - 8x + 4

Taking 4 common: 4(x² - 2x + 1)

x² - 2x + 1 = (x - 1)²

4x² - 8x + 4 = 4(x - 1)²

Answer: 4(x - 1)²

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle 4 common liya, phir bracket ke andar perfect square hai. (x - 1)² identity use karke final answer 4(x - 1)² mila.

(vi) 121b² - 88bc + 16c²

This is in the form (a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b²

Here a = 11b, b = 4c

121b² - 88bc + 16c² = (11b - 4c)²

Answer: (11b - 4c)²

Hinglish Explanation: Ye perfect square hai. (a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b² identity use karke, a = 11b aur b = 4c rakhne par (11b - 4c)² milta hai.

(vii) (l + m)² - 4lm

(l + m)² - 4lm = l² + 2lm + m² - 4lm

= l² - 2lm + m²

= (l - m)²

Answer: (l - m)²

Hinglish Explanation: (l + m)² ko expand karke 4lm subtract kiya. Result perfect square (l - m)² ban gaya.

(viii) a⁴ + 2a²b² + b⁴

This is in the form (a² + b²)² = a⁴ + 2a²b² + b⁴

a⁴ + 2a²b² + b⁴ = (a² + b²)²

Answer: (a² + b²)²

Hinglish Explanation: Ye perfect square hai. (a² + b²)² identity use karke directly factorise kar diya.

Question 2: Factorise:

Solution:

(i) 4p² - 9q²

This is in the form a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b)

Here a = 2p, b = 3q

4p² - 9q² = (2p + 3q)(2p - 3q)

Answer: (2p + 3q)(2p - 3q)

Hinglish Explanation: Ye difference of squares hai. a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b) identity use karke, a = 2p aur b = 3q rakhne par (2p + 3q)(2p - 3q) mila.

(ii) 63a² - 112b²

Taking 7 common: 7(9a² - 16b²)

9a² - 16b² = (3a)² - (4b)²

(3a + 4b)(3a - 4b)

63a² - 112b² = 7(3a + 4b)(3a - 4b)

Answer: 7(3a + 4b)(3a - 4b)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle 7 common liya, phir bracket ke andar difference of squares hai. a² - b² identity use karke final answer 7(3a + 4b)(3a - 4b) mila.

(iii) 49x² - 36

This is in the form a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b)

Here a = 7x, b = 6

49x² - 36 = (7x + 6)(7x - 6)

Answer: (7x + 6)(7x - 6)

Hinglish Explanation: Ye difference of squares hai. a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b) identity use karke, a = 7x aur b = 6 rakhne par (7x + 6)(7x - 6) mila.

(iv) 16x⁵ - 144x³

Taking 16x³ common: 16x³(x² - 9)

x² - 9 = (x + 3)(x - 3)

16x⁵ - 144x³ = 16x³(x + 3)(x - 3)

Answer: 16x³(x + 3)(x - 3)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle 16x³ common liya, phir bracket ke andar difference of squares hai. x² - 9 = (x + 3)(x - 3) identity use karke final answer mila.

(v) (l + m)² - (l - m)²

This is in the form a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b)

Here a = (l + m), b = (l - m)

(l + m)² - (l - m)² = [(l + m) + (l - m)][(l + m) - (l - m)]

= [l + m + l - m][l + m - l + m]

= [2l][2m] = 4lm

Answer: 4lm

Hinglish Explanation: Ye difference of squares hai. a² - b² identity use karke simplify karne par 4lm mila.

(vi) 9x²y² - 16

This is in the form a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b)

Here a = 3xy, b = 4

9x²y² - 16 = (3xy + 4)(3xy - 4)

Answer: (3xy + 4)(3xy - 4)

Hinglish Explanation: Ye difference of squares hai. a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b) identity use karke, a = 3xy aur b = 4 rakhne par (3xy + 4)(3xy - 4) mila.

(vii) (x² - 2xy + y²) - z²

x² - 2xy + y² = (x - y)²

(x - y)² - z² = [(x - y) + z][(x - y) - z]

= (x - y + z)(x - y - z)

Answer: (x - y + z)(x - y - z)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle x² - 2xy + y² ko (x - y)² mein convert kiya, phir difference of squares identity use karke final answer mila.

(viii) 25a² - 4b² + 28bc - 49c²

Rearranging: 25a² - (4b² - 28bc + 49c²)

4b² - 28bc + 49c² = (2b)² - 2(2b)(7c) + (7c)² = (2b - 7c)²

25a² - (2b - 7c)² = (5a)² - (2b - 7c)²

= [5a + (2b - 7c)][5a - (2b - 7c)]

= (5a + 2b - 7c)(5a - 2b + 7c)

Answer: (5a + 2b - 7c)(5a - 2b + 7c)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle terms ko rearrange kiya, phir perfect square aur difference of squares identities use karke final answer mila.

Question 4: Factorise:

Solution:

(i) a⁴ - b⁴

(a²)² - (b²)² = (a² + b²)(a² - b²)

= (a² + b²)(a + b)(a - b)

Answer: (a² + b²)(a + b)(a - b)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle difference of squares identity use karke (a² + b²)(a² - b²) likha, phir a² - b² ko fir se difference of squares identity use karke (a + b)(a - b) likha.

(ii) p⁴ - 81

(p²)² - (9)² = (p² + 9)(p² - 9)

= (p² + 9)(p + 3)(p - 3)

Answer: (p² + 9)(p + 3)(p - 3)

Hinglish Explanation: Pehle difference of squares identity use karke (p² + 9)(p² - 9) likha, phir p² - 9 ko fir se difference of squares identity use karke (p + 3)(p - 3) likha.

(iii) x⁴ - (y + z)⁴

(x²)² - [(y + z)²]²

= [x² + (y + z)²][x² - (y + z)²]

= [x² + (y + z)²][x + (y + z)][x - (y + z)]

= [x² + (y + z)²][x + y + z][x - y - z]

Answer: [x² + (y + z)²][x + y + z][x - y - z]

Hinglish Explanation: Difference of squares identity use karke step by step factorise kiya.

(iv) x⁴ - (x - z)⁴

(x²)² - [(x - z)²]²

= [x² + (x - z)²][x² - (x - z)²]

= [x² + (x - z)²][x + (x - z)][x - (x - z)]

= [x² + (x - z)²][2x - z][z]

Answer: z(2x - z)[x² + (x - z)²]

Hinglish Explanation: Difference of squares identity use karke step by step factorise kiya.

(v) a⁴ - 2a²b² + b⁴

This is in the form (a²)² - 2(a²)(b²) + (b²)²

= (a² - b²)²

= [(a + b)(a - b)]²

= (a + b)²(a - b)²

Answer: (a + b)²(a - b)²

Hinglish Explanation: Ye perfect square hai. (a² - b²)² likhne ke baad, a² - b² ko difference of squares identity use karke (a + b)(a - b) likha, phir square kiya.

Question 5: Factorise the following expressions:

Solution:

(i) p² + 6p + 8

We need two numbers whose sum is 6 and product is 8

4 and 2: 4 + 2 = 6, 4 × 2 = 8

p² + 6p + 8 = p² + 4p + 2p + 8

= p(p + 4) + 2(p + 4)

= (p + 4)(p + 2)

Answer: (p + 4)(p + 2)

Hinglish Explanation: Middle term ko split kiya. Aise do numbers dhoonde jinka sum 6 aur product 8 ho. 4 aur 2 numbers the. Phir grouping karke factorise kiya.

(ii) q² - 10q + 21

We need two numbers whose sum is -10 and product is 21

-7 and -3: -7 + (-3) = -10, (-7) × (-3) = 21

q² - 10q + 21 = q² - 7q - 3q + 21

= q(q - 7) - 3(q - 7)

= (q - 7)(q - 3)

Answer: (q - 7)(q - 3)

Hinglish Explanation: Middle term ko split kiya. Aise do numbers dhoonde jinka sum -10 aur product 21 ho. -7 aur -3 numbers the. Phir grouping karke factorise kiya.

(iii) p² + 6p - 16

We need two numbers whose sum is 6 and product is -16

8 and -2: 8 + (-2) = 6, 8 × (-2) = -16

p² + 6p - 16 = p² + 8p - 2p - 16

= p(p + 8) - 2(p + 8)

= (p + 8)(p - 2)

Answer: (p + 8)(p - 2)

Hinglish Explanation: Middle term ko split kiya. Aise do numbers dhoonde jinka sum 6 aur product -16 ho. 8 aur -2 numbers the. Phir grouping karke factorise kiya.

Question 1: Carry out the following divisions:

Solution:

(i) 28x⁴ ÷ 56x

= (28/56) × (x⁴/x)

= (1/2) × x³

= x³/2

Answer: x³/2

Hinglish Explanation: Coefficient ko divide kiya (28/56 = 1/2) aur variables ko divide kiya (x⁴/x = x³).

(ii) -36y³ ÷ 9y²

= (-36/9) × (y³/y²)

= -4 × y

= -4y

Answer: -4y

Hinglish Explanation: Coefficient ko divide kiya (-36/9 = -4) aur variables ko divide kiya (y³/y² = y).

(iii) 66pq²r³ ÷ 11qr²

= (66/11) × (p/p⁰) × (q²/q) × (r³/r²)

= 6 × p × q × r

= 6pqr

Answer: 6pqr

Hinglish Explanation: Coefficient ko divide kiya (66/11 = 6) aur har variable ko alag-alag divide kiya.

(iv) 34x³y³z³ ÷ 51xy²z³

= (34/51) × (x³/x) × (y³/y²) × (z³/z³)

= (2/3) × x² × y × 1

= (2x²y)/3

Answer: (2x²y)/3

Hinglish Explanation: Coefficient ko divide kiya (34/51 = 2/3) aur har variable ko alag-alag divide kiya.

(v) 12a⁸b⁸ ÷ (-6a⁶b⁴)

= (12/-6) × (a⁸/a⁶) × (b⁸/b⁴)

= -2 × a² × b⁴

= -2a²b⁴

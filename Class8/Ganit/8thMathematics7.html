Question 3: 72% of 25 students are interested in mathematics. How many are not interested in mathematics?

Solution:

Total students = 100%

Interested in mathematics = 72%

Not interested in mathematics = 100% - 72% = 28%

Answer: 28%

Hinglish Explanation: Total students 100% hain. 72% mathematics mein interested hain. Toh not interested = 100 - 72 = 28%.

Question 4: A football team won 10 matches out of the total number of matches they played. If their win percentage was 40, then how many matches did they play in all?

Solution:

Let total number of matches = x

Matches won = 10

Win percentage = 40%

Therefore, 40% of x = 10

(

40 100
) × x = 10

x = 10 × (

100 40
) = 10 × 2.5 = 25

Answer: 25 matches

Hinglish Explanation: Maan lete hain total matches x hain. 40% of x = 10. Is equation ko solve karenge: (40/100) × x = 10, toh x = 10 × (100/40) = 25.

Question 5: If Chameli had ₹ 600 left after spending 75% of her money, how much did she have in the beginning?

Solution:

Let initial money = ₹ x

Money spent = 75% of x = 0.75x

Money left = x - 0.75x = 0.25x

According to the question, 0.25x = 600

x = 600 / 0.25 = 600 × 4 = 2400

Answer: ₹ 2400

Hinglish Explanation: Maan lete hain shuruat mein Chameli ke paas ₹x the. Usne 75% kharch kar diya, toh bache 25%. Question ke hisaab se 25% of x = 600, toh x = 600 ÷ 0.25 = 2400.

Question 3: An almirah is sold for ₹ 5225 after allowing a discount of 5%. Find its marked price.

Solution:

Let marked price = ₹ x

Discount = 5%

Selling price = Marked price - Discount = x - 5% of x = x - 0.05x = 0.95x

According to the question, 0.95x = 5225

x = 5225 / 0.95 = 5500

Answer: ₹ 5500

Hinglish Explanation: Maan lete hain marked price ₹x hai. 5% discount ke baad selling price = x - 0.05x = 0.95x. Question ke hisaab se 0.95x = 5225, toh x = 5225 ÷ 0.95 = 5500.

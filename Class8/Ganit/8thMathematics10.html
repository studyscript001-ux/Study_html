Exercise 10.1

Question 1: Evaluate: (i) 3-2 (ii) (-4)-2 (iii) (12)-5

Solution:

(i) 3-2

According to exponent rule: a-m = 1am

3-2 = 13² = 19

Answer: 19

Explanation (Hinglish): Negative exponent ka matlab hai ki hum 1 ko uss number se divide kar rahe hain. Yahan 3-2 ka matlab hai 1 divided by 32 which is 1/9.

(ii) (-4)-2

(-4)-2 = 1(-4)² = 116

Answer: 116

Explanation (Hinglish): Negative exponent rule apply karte hain. (-4)-2 = 1/(-4)2 = 1/16. Even power ke wajah se negative sign nahi raha.

(iii) (12)-5

(12)-5 = (21)5 = 25 = 32

Answer: 32

Explanation (Hinglish): Jab fraction pe negative exponent ho, toh hum fraction ko flip kar dete hain aur exponent positive ho jata hai. (1/2)-5 = (2/1)5 = 25 = 32.

Question 2: Simplify and express the result in positive exponent form:

(i) (-4)5 ÷ (-4)8 (ii) (12³)2 (iii) (-3)4 × (53)4

(iv) (3-7 ÷ 3-10) × 3-5 (v) 2-3 × (-7)-3

Solution:

(i) (-4)5 ÷ (-4)8

According to exponent rule: am ÷ an = am-n

(-4)5-8 = (-4)-3 = 1(-4)³ = -164

Answer: -164

Explanation (Hinglish): Same base ke saath division mein exponents subtract hote hain. (-4)5-8 = (-4)-3 = 1/(-4)3 = -1/64.

(ii) (12³)2

(12³)2 = 1²(2³)² = 12⁶ = 164

Answer: 164

Explanation (Hinglish): Jab hum fraction pe exponent lagate hain, toh numerator aur denominator dono pe exponent apply hota hai. (1/2³)² = 1²/(2³)² = 1/2⁶ = 1/64.

(iii) (-3)4 × (53)4

(-3)4 × (53)4 = [(-3) × (53)]4 = (-5)4 = 625

Answer: 625

Explanation (Hinglish): Same exponent ke saath multiplication mein hum bases ko multiply kar sakte hain. (-3) × (5/3) = -5, then (-5)4 = 625.

(iv) (3-7 ÷ 3-10) × 3-5

3-7 ÷ 3-10 = 3-7-(-10) = 33

33 × 3-5 = 33-5 = 3-2 = 19

Answer: 19

Explanation (Hinglish): Pehle division mein exponents subtract karte hain: -7 - (-10) = 3, so 3³. Phir multiply karte hain: 3³ × 3-5 = 33-5 = 3-2 = 1/9.

(v) 2-3 × (-7)-3

2-3 × (-7)-3 = [2 × (-7)]-3 = (-14)-3 = -12744

Answer: -12744

Explanation (Hinglish): Same negative exponent ke saath hum bases ko multiply kar sakte hain. [2 × (-7)]-3 = (-14)-3 = -1/14³ = -1/2744.

Question 3: Find the value of:

(i) (30 + 4-1) × 22 (ii) (2-1 × 4-1) ÷ 2-2

(iii) (12)-2 + (13)-2 + (14)-2 (iv) (3-1 + 4-1 + 5-1)0

(v) {(-23)-2}2

Solution:

(i) (30 + 4-1) × 22

30 = 1, 4-1 = 14, 22 = 4

(1 + 14) × 4 = (54) × 4 = 5

Answer: 5

Explanation (Hinglish): 30 = 1, 4-1 = 1/4, 22 = 4. (1 + 1/4) = 5/4, then (5/4) × 4 = 5.

(ii) (2-1 × 4-1) ÷ 2-2

2-1 = 12, 4-1 = 14, 2-2 = 14

(12 × 14) ÷ (14) = (18) ÷ (14) = (18) × 4 = 12

Answer: 12

Explanation (Hinglish): 2-1 = 1/2, 4-1 = 1/4, 2-2 = 1/4. (1/2 × 1/4) = 1/8, then 1/8 ÷ 1/4 = 1/8 × 4 = 1/2.

(iii) (12)-2 + (13)-2 + (14)-2

(12)-2 = 22 = 4

(13)-2 = 32 = 9

(14)-2 = 42 = 16

4 + 9 + 16 = 29

Answer: 29

Explanation (Hinglish): Fraction ka negative exponent means reciprocal ka positive exponent. (1/2)-2 = 22 = 4, (1/3)-2 = 32 = 9, (1/4)-2 = 42 = 16. Total = 4+9+16=29.

(iv) (3-1 + 4-1 + 5-1)0

Any number to the power 0 is 1

(3-1 + 4-1 + 5-1)0 = 1

Answer: 1

Explanation (Hinglish): Kisi bhi number ki 0 power 1 hoti hai, chahe wo kuch bhi ho bracket ke andar. Isliye yahan answer 1 hai.

(v) {(-23)-2}2

(-23)-2 = (3-2)2 = 94

{94}2 = 8116

Answer: 8116

Explanation (Hinglish): (-2/3)-2 = (3/-2)2 = 9/4. Phir (9/4)2 = 81/16.

Question 4: Evaluate:

(i) (8-1 × 53)/ 2-4 (ii) (5-1 × 2-1) × 6-1

Solution:

(i) (8-1 × 5³)/ 2-4

8-1 = 18, 2-4 = 116

(18 × 125) ÷ (116) = (1258) × 16 = 125 × 2 = 250

Answer: 250

Explanation (Hinglish): 8-1 = 1/8, 53 = 125, 2-4 = 1/16. (1/8 × 125) = 125/8, then (125/8) ÷ (1/16) = (125/8) × 16 = 125 × 2 = 250.

(ii) (5-1 × 2-1) × 6-1

5-1 = 15, 2-1 = 12, 6-1 = 16

(15 × 12) × 16 = (110) × 16 = 160

Answer: 160

Explanation (Hinglish): 5-1 = 1/5, 2-1 = 1/2, 6-1 = 1/6. (1/5 × 1/2) = 1/10, then 1/10 × 1/6 = 1/60.

Question 5: Find the value of m for which 5m ÷ 5-3 = 55

Solution:

5m ÷ 5-3 = 55

According to exponent rule: 5m-(-3) = 55

5m+3 = 55

Since bases are same, exponents must be equal:

m + 3 = 5

m = 5 - 3 = 2

Answer: m = 2

Explanation (Hinglish): Same base ke saath division mein exponents subtract hote hain. 5m ÷ 5-3 = 5m-(-3) = 5m+3. Yahan 5m+3 = 55, so m+3=5, m=2.

Question 6: Evaluate:

(i) {(13)-1 - (14)-1}-1

(ii) (58)-7 × (85)-5

Solution:

(i) {(13)-1 - (14)-1}-1

(13)-1 = 3, (14)-1 = 4

{(3 - 4)}-1 = (-1)-1 = -1

Answer: -1

Explanation (Hinglish): (1/3)-1 = 3, (1/4)-1 = 4. (3-4) = -1, then (-1)-1 = -1.

(ii) (58)-7 × (85)-5

(58)-7 = (85)7

(85)7 × (85)-5 = (85)7-5 = (85)2 = 6425

Answer: 6425

Explanation (Hinglish): (5/8)-7 = (8/5)7. Same base (8/5) ke saath multiplication mein exponents add hote hain: (8/5)7 × (8/5)-5 = (8/5)7-5 = (8/5)2 = 64/25.

Question 7: Simplify:

(i) 25 × t-4 / 5-3 × 10 × t-8 (t ≠ 0)

(ii) 3-5 × 10-5 × 125 / 5-7 × 6-5

Solution:

(i) 25 × t-4 / 5-3 × 10 × t-8

25 = 52, 10 = 2 × 5

52 × t-4 / 5-3 × 2 × 5 × t-8

= 52 × t-4 / 5-2 × 2 × t-8

= 52-(-2) × t-4-(-8) /2

= 54 × t4 / 2 = 625t4/2

Answer: 625t4/2

Explanation (Hinglish): 25 = 5², 10 = 2×5. Same base ke saath exponents subtract karte hain: 52-(-2) = 54 = 625, t-4-(-8) = t4. Final answer 625t4/2.

(ii) 3-5 × 10-5 × 125 / 5-7 × 6-5

10 = 2 × 5, 125 = 53, 6 = 2 × 3

3-5 × (2×5)-5 × 53 / 5-7 × (2×3)-5

= 3-5 × 2-5 × 5-5 × 53 / 5-7 × 2-5 × 3-5

= 5-5+3-(-7) = 55 = 3125

Answer: 3125

Explanation (Hinglish): 10 = 2×5, 125 = 5³, 6 = 2×3. Same bases ke exponents cancel ho jate hain. Sirf 5 ka exponent bachta hai: -5+3-(-7) = 5, so 55 = 3125.

Question 4: In a stack there are 5 books each of thickness 20 mm and 5 paper sheets each of thickness 0.016 mm. What is the total thickness of the stack?

Solution:

Total thickness of 5 books = 5 × 20 = 100 mm

Total thickness of 5 paper sheets = 5 × 0.016 = 0.08 mm

Total thickness of stack = 100 + 0.08 = 100.08 mm

In standard form = 1.0008 × 102 mm

Answer: 100.08 mm or 1.0008 × 102 mm

Explanation (Hinglish): 5 books × 20 mm = 100 mm. 5 paper sheets × 0.016 mm = 0.08 mm. Total = 100 + 0.08 = 100.08 mm. Standard form mein = 1.0008 × 102 mm.

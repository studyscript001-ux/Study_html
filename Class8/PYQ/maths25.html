Multiple Choice Questions (Q.1 to 5)

Instructions- Choose and write the correct option. Each question carries 1 mark.

Q.1 The value of 52 + 62 = ? is-
(A) 112
(B) 52
(C) 62
(D) 114
Solution: 52 + 62 = 5+62 = 112
So, the correct option is (A) 112
Q.2 The linear equation in one variable is -
(A) 2x+3=0
(B) 2x+y=0
(C) x=4
(D) xy=3
Solution: A linear equation in one variable has only one variable with power 1.
(A) 2x+3=0 - has one variable x
(B) 2x+y=0 - has two variables
(C) x=4 - has one variable x
(D) xy=3 - has two variables and is not linear
Both (A) and (C) are correct, but typically x=4 is considered the simplest form.
So, the correct option is (C) x=4
Q.3 The measure of each exterior angle of a regular polygon having 8 sides is -
(A) 90°
(B) 54°
(C) 45°
(D) 24°
Solution: Sum of all exterior angles of any polygon = 360°
For a regular polygon with 8 sides, each exterior angle = 360°8 = 45°
So, the correct option is (C) 45°
Q.4 Square root of 169 is-
(A) 9
(B) 10
(C) 13
(D) 16
Solution: 13 × 13 = 169
So, √169 = 13
So, the correct option is (C) 13
Q.5 If x is a non zero integer then the value of x⁻¹ is-
(A) x
(B) 1x
(C) -x
(D) -1x
Solution: By definition, x⁻¹ = 1x
So, the correct option is (B) 1x

Very Short Answer Type Questions (Q. 11 to 16)

Instructions- Solve the following questions. Each question carries 2 marks.

Q.11 If the measure of one of the adjacent angle of a parallelogram is 120° then find the measure of the other adjacent angle.
Solution: In a parallelogram, adjacent angles are supplementary (sum = 180°).
If one angle is 120°, then the adjacent angle = 180° - 120° = 60°.

Solution: When speed is constant, distance is directly proportional to time.

Q.16 If the unit digit of a perfect square number is 9 then what will be the unit digit of its square root?
Solution: If the unit digit of a perfect square is 9, then the unit digit of its square root will be 3 or 7.

Q.21 Find the length of a side of a cube the volume of which is 125 cm³
Solution: Volume of cube = side³
side³ = 125
side = ∛125 = 5 cm
So, the length of each side is 5 cm.
Q.22 After spending 75% of her money, if Chameli is left with ₹600, then find how much money she had in the beginning?
Solution: Let the initial money be ₹x
Money spent = 75% of x = 0.75x
Money left = x - 0.75x = 0.25x
0.25x = 600
x = 6000.25 = ₹2400
So, she had ₹2400 in the beginning.

Q.23 Subtract 5x² - 4y² + 6y - 3 from 7x² - 4xy + 8y² + 5x - 3y
Solution:
(7x² - 4xy + 8y² + 5x - 3y) - (5x² - 4y² + 6y - 3)
= 7x² - 4xy + 8y² + 5x - 3y - 5x² + 4y² - 6y + 3
= (7x² - 5x²) - 4xy + (8y² + 4y²) + 5x + (-3y - 6y) + 3
= 2x² - 4xy + 12y² + 5x - 9y + 3
Q.24 The area is 240, its diagonal is 16 cm long, then find the length of its other diagonal.
Solution: Assuming this is a rhombus (since both diagonals are mentioned):
Area of rhombus = 12 × d₁ × d₂
240 = 12 × 16 × d₂
240 = 8 × d₂
d₂ = 2408 = 30 cm
So, the other diagonal is 30 cm long.
Q.25 Factorize: z - 7 + 7xy - xyz
Solution: z - 7 + 7xy - xyz
= (z - 7) + (7xy - xyz)
= (z - 7) + xy(7 - z)
= (z - 7) - xy(z - 7)
= (z - 7)(1 - xy)
Q.26 Draw graph representing the information in the table given below
Side of a square (in cm) 2 3 4 5
Area (in sq. cm.) 4 9 16 25
Solution: This represents a quadratic relationship y = x²
To draw the graph:
- Mark points (2,4), (3,9), (4,16), (5,25) on the coordinate plane
- Join these points with a smooth curve
- The graph will be a parabola opening upwards
- The curve will pass through the origin (0,0)
